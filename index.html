~Before Fame~

Billie Eilish was born into a family of artists ad actors on December 18, 2001 in Los Angeles, California.She got into singing because she would sing along with the Los Angeles Children's Chorusduring her childhood. At the age of 11, Billie started to write her own songs for her to sing. Growing up, Billie loved to listen to her favorite artists Justin Bieber. In a radio intervie with KROQ, she says, "That was like my first love. That was the person I was in love with, in my head he was in love with me, it was like a relationship with a person." She soon followed in to the footsteps of other family members by being an artist.


~The Journey~

Billie Eilish is a 17 year old electropop artist who first got famous from her 2017 singles "Ocean Eyes" which was originally written by her brother Finneas in 2015 and "Fingers Crossed". She continued her career after her song "Ocean Eyes" got more than 51 million streams on Spotify as of May 2018.. Billie gained many fans with her first two songs which encouraged her to write more songs.Soo, her singles of the "WHEN WE FALL ASLEEP WHERE DO WE GO" and "Don't Smile At Me" album have beaten famous artists such a Ariana Grande on the pop charts.
